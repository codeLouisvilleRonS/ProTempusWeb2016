Secure non cloud based software

ProTempus is a secure, cloudless law office practice management software for solo and small law firms. Our unique technology keeps your data on your local network, NOT floating on the cloud. Your authorized staff accesses data through your sharable database without any expensive hardware upgrades.

ProTempus is a world-class, comprehensive system that embodies quick time capture, precise billing, expanded contact and party management, real-time remote access, secure client information, document management and more.

With ProTempus, you share your data with your staff -- not with the world!

More Green on your screen means more green in your pocket!

NO MORE PROBONO OVER THE PHONE!

Our unique integration of billing and your daily calendar make it easier to document cases, schedule time and bill time all from the same window. With two clicks, an event turns into an invoice item. When a call happens, one click turns it into an invoice item. The ProTempus stop watch will even track the time you are on the phone call.

Increase your Billables by 25%

ProTempus is the only law office software that gives you an instant snapshot of billable time, minute by minute, throughout the day. When you check billable on a phone call, a meeting or the time you spend drafting a document or an email, ProTempus displays that event on your daily calendar in GREEN. At the end of the day, you can easily view your billable time. It’s easy to spot omissions.

Capturing every ten minute phone call can easily increase your billable time by 25% and increase you customer service through better documentation.

Invoicing done the right way, YOUR WAY!

ProTempus has you covered.

Whether you bill monthly, quarterly, once a case is completed, up front or ad-hoc, ProTempus makes invoicing easy. A few mouse clicks generate invoices regardless of your billing cycle. We import your office letterhead. You can create virtually unlimited detail on each line item describing exactly what was done on the case. ProTempus attorneys realize improved pay rates and reduced Accounts Receivable when details are included on invoices.

Your client’s choice.

Invoice options are traditional mail or email and can be set to each client’s preference. Attorneys check the invoices before printing if they choose. Once generated, invoices print on your local printer. These invoices have a folding line to make it easy to stuff into window envelopes. A follow up step allows you to step through the invoices that need to be emailed, allowing you to customize the email message for individual clients.

Fully Integrated Accounts Receivables

Post payments quickly.

If you know a client is behind on payments, your next meeting or phone call allows you to handle the matter. Fully integrated Trust Accounting

ProTempus manages your trust accounts.

ProTempus has a fully integrated Trust Accounting subsystem. When you receive a deposit, you can easily put that amount into the client’s Trust Account. When you create invoices for your clients you can either automatically apply money from their trust account against the new invoice or selectively apply the trust money as the case is completed. Two clicks will display the client’s current trust account balance. Our billing detail report, account summary on the client’s invoice and numerous audit reports keep you and your clients on top of your trust accounts.

Make audits easy.

If you are using the QuickBooks interface, ProTempus automatically creates a sub account in the QuickBooks Trust registry for each client. It becomes very easy to determine the balance of each client’s trust account. ProTempus will display the balance within ProTempus and QuickBooks also displays their balance in their individual sub account within your trust bank account. You have the option of maintaining your trust bank as a separate QuickBooks company file. This method allows you to optionally give the Bar Association Auditor the QuickBooks trust account without sharing your complete financial data.

Complete contact management system.

Keep track of all contacts, not just clients.

All contacts are valuable, not just clients. If you enter everyone into ProTempus, ProTempus provides instant conflict checks. You can enter multiple addresses, multiple phone numbers and unlimited notes for each contact. Linking contacts to a given case gives you instant access to all parties in a case, including the judges, opposing attorneys, witnesses, adjusters, etc., with one click. Contact management is the central point of ProTempus. Any lawyer or authorized staff in your office can view any case that includes the contact or any history with the office.

Instant conflict checking.

When adding a new client into ProTempus, it displays all contacts with that same last name. This feature prevents duplicates. In addition it provides you with the history of that contact to prevent possible conflicts of interest. This is especially true in offices with more than one lawyer. ProTempus is the common repository for your entire office. With ProTempus the right hand knows what the left hand is doing. Allowing you to provide the best possible customer service to your clients, while solving their legal issues. ProTempus software takes the pain out of running a law office.

Case management made easy.

ProTempus will manage the details.

Documents, trial dates, deadlines, emails sent and received, phone calls, correspondence from opposing attorney, time billed, time to be invoiced, payments received, Trust account balance, tracking parties in the case, are just some of features ProTempus tracks on each case. Each of these is just one mouse click away once you select the client. ProTempus saves you and your staff hours each week from the details of managing each case.

Track all the parties in a case.

Managing and communicating with all of the parties in a case can be a major headache. ProTempus can link any contact in your database to any case, defining their role in a specific case. There is no limit to the number of parties in a case. You may find you are facing an opposing counsel in multiple cases. ProTempus can handle that along with the any details that are specific to that case.

Accessing any document for a given client is one click away.

No more navigating the windows directory structure

Once you select a client, click on the documents button on the client’s windows. A list of all of their documents will appear. Attorneys can group the documents is subfolders by matter code, document type or the most recent at the top. When creating new documents, select a boilerplate first, name your document and ProTempus will automatically place the document in the client’s document folder. No more misplaced documents. Pointing ProTempus to your shared drive means all your staff and attorneys can access any document from any computer.

Document assembly made simple.

ProTempus features built-in document assembly software. You can create a intake form for each type of case. Once information is collected you can generate many documents that merge the intake data with your boilerplate documents. Document creation can be as simple as three mouse clicks.

ProTempus keeps your To Do list manageable.

ProTempus helps attorneys stay on task

By assigning trigger dates to each task item, you can tell ProTempus when you want it to notify you of an upcoming deadline. Lawyers will not have any last minute surprises with ProTempus. Many systems overwhelm their users by displaying all of your tasks on your current To Do list. By default ProTempus will only display tasks that have a trigger date on or prior to the current date. When you come to the office each morning, ProTempus focuses you on the tasks that need to be completed today. You have the option to look ahead to view tasks that will be due next week or next month.

ProTempus can track the time you spend on a task

Our To Do list also integrates with our billing system. When a task is completed, one click adds it to the client’s next invoice. You can accumulate the time you worked on a task over a period of time whether it takes a week or a day. Starting the stop watch each time you work on a task accumulates the total time you spent. Once completed, click billable and the accumulated time appears on the client’s invoice.

ProTempus has an optional interface with QuickBooks.

We put the Quick back into QuickBooks

Many ProTempus users find that the Accounts Receivable sub system in ProTempus eliminates the need to use QuickBooks. Others like QuickBooks to help balance their bank accounts and to generate the general ledger for year-end tax reports. We have designed a very simple interface with QuickBooks. When you post a payment or deposit into ProTempus, our sync program adds those deposits into the appropriate check register in QuickBooks. Payments are added as deposits into your operations accounts, prepayments are added as deposits into the client’s sub account in your trust account check register.

Auditing made easy

ProTempus produces audit reports for each step. It verifies the account totals before and after each update to ensure your accounts are in balance. The trust account report in ProTempus has an optional audit feature that retrieves the balance of each client’s from QuickBooks and verifies that the total matches the trust account balance in ProTempus. You can optionally segregate your trust account into a separate QuickBooks company file that allows auditors access to your trust account detail without sharing all of your office’s financial data.

Google sync

Syncing your ProTempus Calendar with your Google Calendar

Our Google sync enables your ProTempus contacts and your Google contacts to sync quickly between the ProTempus calendar and your Google calendar. When you sync your smart phone with your Google contacts and calendar, ProTempus is the ultimate device for calendar management and contact access when you are out of the office. No ports need to be opened to make this sync work, which keeps your office data secure.

Billing time when you are out of the office

Our Google sync also allows you to bill time. Assume you placed a call to John Decker when you are out of the office and you need to bill thirty minutes for that call. Click on your Google calendar to create a new event for the current date and time. Touch the microphone and dictate, “Bill John Decker .5 hours; discussed custody of the children.” Save the event. That’s it. When you return to your office ProTempus billable that event on your calendar. You can also add a meeting with John by selecting the correct date and time on your Google calendar by typing or dictating “Schedule meeting with John Decker to discuss custody of the children.” When you return to your office that event is on your ProTempus calendar.

Outlook sync

Bill your time and schedule meetings from your smart phone

Bill your time and schedule meetings from your smart phone If your smart phone currently syncs with your Outlook calendar, you do not need to change. ProTempus also syncs with Outlook. The same features described in the Google sync also work with Outlook.

Archive you Outlook emails into the client’s ProTempus Document folder

Managing emails can be as big a headache as managing documents. ProTempus comes to the rescue. Select one or more emails in your Outlook inbox, then select a client in ProTempus and click on the email import button. ProTempus archives those emails into the client’s document folder. Once archived into ProTempus, the email is viewable by anyone in your office. It is also included in your normal backups, giving you a permanent record of all emails from the client you archived.

You can also archive email from Gmail accounts by adding your Gmail account to Outlook.

Secure Limited Remote Access

Remote access is possible without moving your data to the cloud!

The ProTempus Google calendar and Outlook syncs described above insulate your systems from the WORLD WILD WEB. When you need to access your documents or other software while out of the office, there are several remote access programs that provide remote access without opening ports on your firewall. TeamViewer, Splashtop and LogMeIn all work well with Protempus. Teamviewer is the fastest and has a free version for occasional usage. Windows remote desktop (RDC) will also work; however it requires an open port on your firewall, it should be setup by a professional that can configure and lock down your firewall, to your specific IP addresses.
